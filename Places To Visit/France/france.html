FRANCE

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. With an official estimated population of 2,102,650 residents as of 1 January 2023 in an area of more than 105 km2 (41 sq mi), Paris is the fourth-most populated city in the European Union and the 30th most densely populated city in the world in 2022. Since the 17th century, Paris has been one of the world's major centres of finance, diplomacy, commerce, culture, fashion, and gastronomy. For its leading role in the arts and sciences, as well as its early and extensive system of street lighting, in the 19th century, it became known as the City of Light.

For me, visiting Paris, France, is a lifelong dream filled with endless allure and fascination. There are countless reasons why people flock to this iconic city, but for me, it's the perfect blend of history, culture, romance, and culinary delights that make it an irresistible destination. First and foremost, Paris is a treasure trove of historical landmarks and architectural marvels. From the majestic Eiffel Tower, symbolizing the city's grandeur and elegance, to the awe-inspiring Notre-Dame Cathedral, each corner seems to whisper tales of centuries past. Exploring the charming cobblestone streets of Le Marais or wandering through the magnificent halls of the Louvre Museum, where masterpieces like the Mona Lisa reside, fills me with a sense of wonder and reverence for the city's rich cultural heritage. Moreover, Paris is synonymous with romance, and there's something undeniably enchanting about strolling hand in hand along the Seine River or picnicking beneath the shade of the iconic cherry blossoms in Parc des Buttes-Chaumont. Watching the sunset from the steps of Montmartre or sharing a kiss beneath the twinkling lights of the Eiffel Tower is the stuff of dreams.
